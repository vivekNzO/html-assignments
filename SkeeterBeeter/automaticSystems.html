AUTOMATIC SYSTEMS

AUTOMATIC MOSQUITO SYSTEMS

For some properties, spraying ocassionally is not enough to rid the area of mosquitoes and stinging insects.

Our automatic mosquito systems are designed to combine our mosquito barrier spray with an automatic misting system for full protection.

The Benefits of Our Automatic Systems Include:
  • Constant and reliable treatment
  • Automatic release of misting treatment
  • The ability to remotely control it
  • Stealth design
  • Automatic refills

A TARGETED AND AUTOMATIC APPROACH

Our automatic systems release a mist in 30-second intervals two to four times daily to constantly target the insects in your yard. Each system is remotely controlled, so it is quick and convenient. And we make sure that you have an ongoing supply so all you have to do is sit back itch- and disease-free.

IT'S YOUR YARD - KICK OUT UNINVITED GUESTS!

Skeeter Beeter will install the automatic system in the most appropriate and targeted area, making sure that it is as inconspicuous as possible.

We also refill your system as needed and we come out to winterize it so it will be good to go at the first sign of hatching larvae. We offer a 100% guarantee, so if you are unhappy with the service at any time, we will do what we can to fix the problem. Our automatic systems are also covered by a one-year warranty to ensure that they are working optimally to keep your family safe!
