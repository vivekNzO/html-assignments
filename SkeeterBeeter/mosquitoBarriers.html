BARRIER SPRAY

OUR MOSQUITO BARRIER PUTS AN INVISIBLE SAFETY BARRIER BETWEEN BUGS AND YOUR FAMILY, WORKS IMMEDIATELY, AND LASTS FOR WEEKS.

SKEETER BEETER TECHNICIANS

The Skeeter Beeter barrier service provides a barrier around your yard using a water-based mist spray that eradicates mosquitoes for weeks. Our technicians are equipped with targeted backpack sprayers that are designed to beat mosquitoes where they live in plants, trees, and shrubs and kill them on contact.

The mist spray barrier is formulated to bond to plants and foliage where it repels mosquitoes for weeks, preventing adult mosquitoes from taking up residence and creating new little biters. It only takes 30 minutes for your yard and your family to be fully protected.

WHAT IS MOSQUITO BARRIER SPRAY?

The barrier spray that Skeeter Beeter uses is a synthetic pyrethroid, which is derived from chrysanthemums. The compounds of the chrysanthemum have insecticidal properties that can be used as a defense against mosquitoes.

The same pyrethroids are currently used in commercial applications meant to control insects like pet shampoos, insecticides, and lice shampoos. The barrier spray that we use is often enough to kill pests on contact including ticks, fleas, and sometimes gnats and files, making it a perfect anti-bug barrier for nearly all insects.

When you hire Skeeter Beeter, we spray your entier outdoor area, focusing primarily on the places where insects hide in shrubs, trees, wood piles, leaves, debris, and tall grass. The spray instantly eliminates insects and binds to foliage where the residue remains a repellent for up to three weeks. Therefore, just one application is like adding an invisible bubble around you and your family.

HOW IS MOSQUITO BARRIER SPRAY USED?

The mosquito barrier spray is naturally made, but it is also a very powerful insecticide.

Althought it's completely safe for humans and animals, we recommend that you allow the spray to dry for a minimum of 30 minutes before returning to treated areas.

Our barrier spray is great for recreational and commercial areas as well. It is one of the best forms of combat that we have in our arsenal to keep people safe from the viruses and diseases that insects can transmit.

WHY CHOOSE SKEETER BEETER?

Skeeter Beeter's patented formula works to kill biting mosquitoes and to stop the transmission of disease.

It is not only safe; it is effective and kills insects on contact. Once dry, it provides a barrier that will keep your outdoor areas safe for the hottest months of the year so your family can safely enjoy time outdoors. After the three weeks have passed, we come back to reapply the barrier.

Our technicians also inspect your property to spot signs of active larvae so that we can treat them directly and identify areas of your property that pose ideal breeding conditions like standing or pooling water. We will return every three weeks to reapply and reinspect as long as you want us to in order to keep your yard insect-free-year-round!

Even after your property is treated, if the areas that attract and breed mosquitoes are not addressed, they can still pose a hazard for insects to multiply and remain, and if your neighbors are not taking the same care as you, their issues might be a beacon for insects to break your yard's barrier. That's why getting everyone on board to eliminate mosquitoes from public areas is the most responsible way to keep everyone safe.

DO YOU OFFER A NATURAL BARRIER SPRAY?

We not only offer our specially formulated synthetic pyrethroid spray; we have a natural mosquito control service that uses other botanicals and essential oils like rosemary, lemongrass, and peppermint to repel and kill biting insects.
